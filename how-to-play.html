Welcome to Corona Spin!

The slot machine that tests your luck to survive the corona virus pandemic. You start giving us your name, for our records, and the year you were born. This is because your age sets your initial score to the game. The older you are, less points you get at the start and... well, you have a smaller chance to survive (any resemblance to the real world?).

The minimum cost for each spin is 2 points. You can increase your bet on the x2 x4 x6 x8 and x10 multiplier buttons. If you increase your bet, the cost for each spin will be multiplied by the value you chose. They cost more, but they also multiply the points you get when you win! For Corona Spin, the order of your slots is very important. Unlike other slot games, each Corona Spin slot represent an action; the combination you get can make you win or lose depending on their placement!

Stay safe and have fun!

Slots in the game:

Mask

Rarity: Very Rare
Good Action. The mask is the item that protects you the most in this game. If it appears along bad actions slots it can save you. If it appears along good actions it brings you a nice bonus.

Gathering

Rarity: Rare
Bad action. Gatherings and not respecting social distance will always make you lose the game, independent of its placement. The only slot that can turn the result to a win would be the use of the mask.

Alcohol

Rarity: Commom
Good action. Alcohol can give you points if it appears after the cough or before the touching face action. If combined with the action of washing hands and/or the use of the mask, it can give you a great bonus!

Washing hands

Rarity: Commom
Good action. Washing hands can save you from losing the game if it appears after the cough action or before the touching face action. If combined with the alcohol slot and/or the use of the mask, it can give you a great bonus!

Cough

Rarity: Commom
Bad action. Coughing can be deadly if followed by the touching face action. But you can still get a good score if it's followed by a good action. But if you get a cough on the last slot, you lose.

Touching face

Rarity: Commom
Bad action. Touching face action can be deadly, specially after the cough action. You can still get a good score if it's preceded by a good action. But if you get a touching face action on the first slot, you lose.
